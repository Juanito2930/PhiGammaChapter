In Fall 2024, Phi-Gamma at Cameron University achieved a record-breaking semester. During Fall Rush Week, over 100 students attended the Annual Jake Christian Concert Cookout, along with other successful events like intramural sports and info-nights. These efforts brought in 23 pledges for the Beta Pledge Class, the largest single-semester pledge class since the chapter's founding in April 2023. The Beta Pledge Class was historic, introducing leadership roles like Pledge Class President and Treasurer, held by Aiden and Carson Chambers. Under Pledge-Master Hank Banner, the class became the first in chapter history to achieve a 100% pass rate on the final history test. On November 1, 2024, the chapter initiated 18 new brothers in a single day, doubling its size. The chapter celebrates this growth and looks forward to the future contributions of its new members.
